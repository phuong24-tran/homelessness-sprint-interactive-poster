How the EWSS Pathway works

Three core components work together as a continuous pathway from early detection to coordinated support.

1. Early mental-health screening
Screen
  • Brief, validated screening tools for students aged 9‚Äì12.
  • Run periodically in class with clear consent & confidentiality protocols.
  • Flags early signs of distress before disengagement or dropout.

2. Referral pathway

Equity Lens

Designed to work with marginalized youth, not watch over them

EWSS is not a surveillance tool. It is co-designed to be culturally safe, trauma-informed, and accessible to the youth who face the highest risk of homelessness.

Tailored supports for families navigating a new country, including language-accessible screening, translated communication, and trusted community partners that reflect cultural backgrounds.
